WHY PEOPLE CHOOSE US.

- From receiving your order, to the preparation of the bouquets to delivery, everything is done by our professional & experienced team. We do not pass your order to other local florists.

- Hand delivery at the doorstep of the receiver. We are experts in delivering to all types of premises including residential, offices, hospitals, weddings/parties, hotels, restaurants, diplomatic embassies, etc.

- We don’t believe in scripts; every query is different and a fresh approach is needed. We love to talk with our customers and solving their queries gives us satisfaction and energy!

- We are an ethical business and always deliver what is promised. Of course, due to the nature of the work we are in, it is sometimes inevitable to substitute which is done in accordance with our policy of using flowers of equal or higher value.

- We have a wide range of flower bouquets available prepared from local as well as imported long stem roses, lilies, orchids, tuberoses, gladiolus, carnations, chrysanthemums, baby’s breath , etc.
